The Hawk Hundred

The Fifth Annual Hawk Hundred is a 100-mile, 50-mile and marathon trail race put on by the Lawrence Trail Hawks. This year’s race takes place on September 12th and 13th in Lawrence, Kansas. The course covers four loops through the rolling hills of Clinton State Park.

As Hawks, we pride ourselves in putting on top-notch events. At The Hawk you’ll find aid stations staffed by experienced ultrarunners and a well-marked course through tough, but pretty trails.
