The Hawk Hundred

The Hawk Hundred is a 100-mile, 50-mile and marathon trail race put on by the Lawrence Trail Hawks. The course covers four loops through the rolling hills of Clinton State Park.

As Hawks, we pride ourselves in putting on top-notch events. At The Hawk you'll find aid stations staffed by experienced ultrarunners and a well-marked course through tough, but pretty trails.

Click through the links above for more information. Any questions? Just e-mail us.

Also be sure to visit the Lawrence Trail Hawks website for information about our weekly group runs as well as our upcoming events.
